Homeade Pretzel

Description

Homeade pretzels just like Grandma used to make

Ingredients

  • 1 cup and 2 tablespoons of water, seperate
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 and a half active dry yeast
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • coarse salt

Instructions

  1. Create bread in bread machine
  2. Form into pretzel shape
  3. Preheat oven and dip pretzels into water and baking soda boiling
  4. Bake pretzels at 425 until golden brown
