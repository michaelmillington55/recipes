General Tsos Chicken Wings

Description

Delicious and spicy for the chinese food fanatic

Ingredients

  • 3 Pounds Chicken Wings
  • Egg wash
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 Tsp of Black Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • 3 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp salt

Sauce Instructions

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seed oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp hot cruched chili paste
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tsp corn starch

Instructions

  1. Combine the flour, black pepper, cayenne pepper, ground giner, and salt together
  2. Prepare an egg wash
  3. Season the chicken wings with salt and pepper, then dip them in the egg wash and cover them in flour.
  4. Cook the wings the way you prefer
  5. Add sauce and enjoy
